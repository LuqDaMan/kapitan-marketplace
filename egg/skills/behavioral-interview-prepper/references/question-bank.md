# Question Bank

## Overview
Master list of behavioral interview questions organized by trait cluster. Use this in Step 3 to select 10-15 questions based on the JD behavioral signals extracted via `behavioral-signals.md`.

## Question Format

Each question is tagged: `[format, difficulty, common-at]`

- **Format:** behavioral / motivational / hypothetical / self-assessment
- **Difficulty:** standard (standalone question) / probing (interviewer follow-up to dig deeper)
- **Common at:** FAANG / quant / AI labs / general

## Trait Cluster Questions

### 1. Leadership & Ownership

- "Tell me about a time you took ownership of a project beyond your defined role." [behavioral, standard, FAANG]
- "Describe a situation where you had to make a critical decision without waiting for consensus." [behavioral, standard, FAANG]
- "Give me an example of when you drove a result end-to-end with minimal oversight." [behavioral, standard, general]
- "Tell me about a time you set direction for your team when the path was unclear." [behavioral, standard, FAANG]
- "Walk me through a time you identified a gap no one else was addressing and took initiative." [behavioral, standard, general]
- "What was the impact, and how did you measure it?" [behavioral, probing, FAANG]
- "What would you have done differently with more resources?" [behavioral, probing, general]

### 2. Collaboration

## Cross-Cutting Categories

### Motivation & Fit

Always include 2-3 of these regardless of trait cluster signals.

- "Why this company?" [motivational, standard, general]
- "Why this role specifically?" [motivational, standard, general]
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?" [motivational, standard, general]
- "Tell me about yourself." [motivational, standard, general]

### Self-Assessment

- "What's your greatest strength?" [self-assessment, standard, general]
- "What's an area you're working to improve?" [self-assessment, standard, general]

### Hypothetical / Situational

- "What would you do if you disagreed with your tech lead's design decision?" [hypothetical, standard, FAANG]
- "How would you prioritize if you had three urgent tasks from different stakeholders?" [hypothetical, standard, general]
- "If you joined a team with significant tech debt and low morale, what would your first 30 days look like?" [hypothetical, standard, FAANG]
- "How would you handle discovering a critical bug one hour before a major launch?" [hypothetical, standard, general]

Use the weighted extraction output from `behavioral-signals.md` to pick 10-15 questions:

1. **Primary clusters** (3+ JD signals): select 2-3 questions per cluster
2. **Secondary clusters** (1-2 JD signals): select 1 question per cluster
3. **Motivation & Fit**: always include at least 2
4. **Hypothetical / Situational**: include 1-2
5. **Probing questions**: do not select as standalone â€” these are interviewer follow-ups that the candidate should anticipate after answering the parent question
